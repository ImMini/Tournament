2 vs 2
Kill Race Tournament

Friday 27th August at 8:30pm UK time

sports_esports

How

Duos are competing against each other in squad lobbies. The hosting team will create the lobby. The team with the most kills during a game will win the round and receive 2 points. In case of a tie 1 point is rewarded each. Once the match is completed, teams will report the outcome on their match in Discord.
